Careers

Join Our Innovative Team

Welcome to Peppermint Robotics, Peppermint is an award-winning robotics company, supported by SINE IIT-Bombay and Qualcomm. Peppermint develops and deploys Industrial and Enterprise robots and platforms for mobility-led services. The Engineering team has 50+ years of experience in building deep-tech and robotics products. Robots built on the Peppermint Platform are deployed across 13 cities in 4 countries!
